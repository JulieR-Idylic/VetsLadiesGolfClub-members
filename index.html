Weekly Signup

How to use the signup sheet:

  • Signing up: Select the SIGNUP link in an open slot, fill out and submit the form.
  • Canceling your time: Select the CANCEL link next to your signup. Submit the form.
  • Changing your time: Select the MODIFY link next to your signup. Pick a new time and submit the form.

The signup sheet opens in a new tab.

Let's Play Ready Golf

  • Be prepared to play when it’s your turn.
  • Limit practice swings; keep pace with the group ahead.
  • Play a provisional when appropriate.
  • Help others look for balls briefly; then move on.
