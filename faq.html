Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about league play, signup, and member expectations.

  • How do I sign up for a tee time?
    Use the Weekly Signup sheet on the Member Home page. Select an open SIGNUP link and submit the form.
  • How do I cancel or change my tee time?
    Use the CANCEL or MODIFY link next to your name on the signup sheet.
  • What if a tee time is full?
    Please choose another available time. Do not overwrite another player’s spot.
  • Who do I contact with questions?
    See the Member Directory for officer contact information.

This page will be updated as new questions come up during the season.
